Donations

Donate Now


Your Green Community family is entirely reliant on donations to deliver its lifesaving emergency services. Every penny makes a difference to our recipients and their families.



  • Post your donation


    We all love a nice surprise in the post and we are no different here at the Green Community family! We appreciate each and every donation we receive, no matter how big or small, and we enjoy reading about all the wonderful ways in which you choose to support us. Please complete a donation form and post it with your cheque made payable to Green Community. Please do not send cash in the post.



  • Bank your donation


    To make a one off donation through your bank account please give our Fundraising Team a call on +639 368 727 409. They will give you the account details and also a reference number for your payment so we know who has sent the donation when it appears on our bank statement. If you would like Green Community to claim the Gift Aid on your donation please download and complete the Gift Aid form.



  • Become one of our Regular Donors


    Many of our supporters choose to donate regularly through a standing order with the bank. If you would like to become a regular donor please download our standing order form here.



  • Gift Aid Declaration


    Your tick makes a difference today!

    Thanks to supporters simply ticking a box North West Air Ambulance claims thousands of pounds though HMRC's Gift Aid scheme. As a registered charity we can claim 25p from the government for every £1 that you donate and it won't cost you a penny! Please download a Gift Aid Declaration form here and send in with your donation. For more information about Gift Aid please take a look at the HMRC website.
